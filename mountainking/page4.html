UNFORGETTABLE DINING EXPERIENCE

Our chef invites you to experience a creative menu of upscale tavern cuisine served in the rustic ambiance of a chic western log cabin. Thirty-foot windows with majestic views of the mountains.

CHEF INSPIRED FOOD, MOUNTAIN INSPIRED MOOD

Enjoy creative local cuisine and jaw-dropping vistas at The View Bar & Grill. Named for its spectacular floor-to-ceiling views of the San Juan Mountains, our restaurant reflects a relaxed alpine ambiance with vaulted timber ceilings and a massive stone fireplace. Share a table and savor elevated tavern fare surrounded by the charm and elegance of a modern mountain lodge.

DINING AT OUR HOTEL IS ALWAYS A SPECIAL EVENT

Our menu evolves seasonally and reflects the simple and straightforward preparation of comfortable classics.

You can experience the best of the culinary culture here, in the restaurants of the Hotel. There are four restaurants and bars to choose from, serving everything from light, informal breakfasts and lunches to exceptional cuisine.

  • Sumptuous Indian and continental meals
  • Veg and non-veg; many fresh vegetables are garden
  • Bed tea, breakfast, lunch, high tea, soup, dinner
  • Well-trained staff to attend to your needs
  • Can accommodate special dietary requests
